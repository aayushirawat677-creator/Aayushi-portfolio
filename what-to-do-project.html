What To Do? — AI-Powered Travel Planner

An intelligent platform that recommends personalized travel experiences based on location, dates, and preferences

Problem Statement

Travelers often miss local experiences because platforms like Google Maps and TripAdvisor emphasize static attractions over timely, personalized events. Users struggle to discover relevant activities that match their specific interests, timing, and location.

Solution

I designed and developed What To Do?, an AI-powered platform that recommends travel experiences based on location, dates, and preferences.

Key Features:

  • AI-driven personalized recommendations
  • Real-time event discovery
  • Occasion-specific filters
  • Radius-based activity search
  • Contextual travel suggestions
